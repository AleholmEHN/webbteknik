 - HSL: 100, 255, 88
 - CMYK: 80,0,98,0
 - PMS 354

 - RGB: 191,191,191
 - HSL: 170,0,191
 - CMYK: 30%svart

Texten i loggan, blå:

 - HSL: 147,255,104
 - CMYK: 90,48,0,0
 - PMS 285
